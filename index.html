Annotation Explorer

A clean and intuitive interface for exploring annotations from our NLP research on factuality in summarization.

What’s in an Annotation?

Each annotation includes a source, a summary, and one or more nonfactual spans in the summary. Each span is rated on four key attributes:

  • Evidence: Is the claim supported by the source?
  • Likelihood: Would a model realistically make this error?
  • Consequence: How severe is the factual inaccuracy?
  • Common Knowledge: Could this be known without reading the source?
