Rating System

Each annotated span includes three key attributes, each rated on a 1-3 scale:

  • Evidence: How well-supported the claim or statement is by available data
  • Likelihood: The probability that the annotated claim or event will occur
  • Consequence: The potential impact or importance of the annotated content
